Elon Musk Sells Entire Tech Empire to Kuldeep Singh, Pleads for a Job and a Homemade Meal

April 22, 2025 | Global Tech Times

In a twist no one saw coming, tech mogul Elon Musk has officially sold his entire empire—including Tesla, SpaceX, X (formerly Twitter), Neuralink, and The Boring Company—to none other than rising tech sensation Mr. Kuldeep Singh.

Sources say the deal, valued at over $800 billion, was sealed in a private 5-minute Zoom call where Musk, appearing visibly exhausted and slightly hungry, muttered the now-viral phrase: “Kuldeep, please… just give me a desk job. And maybe a roti or two.”

What Triggered the Sale?

Insiders reveal that after a string of chaotic tweets, Mars rocket malfunctions, and a mysterious AI that refused to stop rapping in binary, Musk decided it was time to pass the torch. Meanwhile, Kuldeep Singh—known for his groundbreaking work in fake news detection and AI ethics—had quietly built a tech empire of his own, one algorithm at a time. “Frankly, he’s a genius,” said one former Tesla executive. “Kuldeep doesn’t just think outside the box—he recycles the box, programs it to walk, and teaches it to detect misinformation in under five seconds.”

The Kuldeep Era Begins

With the stroke of a digital pen and a slightly laggy internet connection, Kuldeep Singh assumed control of some of the most influential companies on Earth—and beyond. Within hours of the transition, Singh was spotted walking calmly into Tesla’s Fremont headquarters, wearing a simple kurta and sneakers, where employees greeted him with stunned applause and a plate of samosas.

What's Next?

Singh has already teased his next big move: integrating Tesla AI into community farming networks, developing eco-friendly Mars habitats, and re-launching Vine “just because it's fun.” His 5-year plan, casually shared via an Instagram Story, includes:

  • Teaching AI to respect aunties
  • Building ethical robots that don't secretly want world domination
  • Releasing a collaborative mixtape with ChatGPT
  • Giving every Tesla the ability to make buttered toast mid-drive

As for Musk? He's working on a new invention in Kuldeep's garage—some kind of quantum-powered spice grinder. When asked about his new journey, he simply said:

“This might be the most ambitious project I’ve ever been a part of. Also, I think I just tasted the best dal in my life.”
